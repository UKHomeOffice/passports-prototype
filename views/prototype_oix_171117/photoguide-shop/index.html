Go to a booth or shop

What to ask for at a shop

Ask if their photos meet the rules for digital passsport photos. If they do, check that they can give you a digital copy of your photo.

Some shops can also give you a photo code. We'll show you how to use the code to retrieve your digital photo.

What you'll get from a booth

From some booths in the UK and some booths abroad, you'll get a photo code with your printed photos. We'll show you how to use the code to retrieve your digital photo.

The photo code is a new service and may not be available yet from photo booths or shops in your area.
