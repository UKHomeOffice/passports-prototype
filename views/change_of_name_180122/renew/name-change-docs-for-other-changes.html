How did you change your name?

Changing the spelling or order of your first names, or dropping a first name

You need to send:

  • your old passport

And 2 documents that show you're using your new name:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

Other name changes

You need to send:

  • your old passport

And 1 document that proves you changed your name:

  • marriage certificate
  • civil partnership certificate
  • deed poll
  • change of name deed
  • statuary declaration
  • affidavit

And 1 document that shows you're using your new name:

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

If you've changed your name more than once, you'll need to send proof of every name change.


If your documents aren't in English or Welsh you also need to get a certified translation.
