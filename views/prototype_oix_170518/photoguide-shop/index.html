What you'll get from a shop

Afer taking your photo, a shop will usually send you an email with a link to start your application. We can access your digital photo automatically.

Or, your digital photo may be attached to the email. You'll need to save it to the device you're using to apply so that you can upload it.

Remember to ask the assistant in the shop if their photos meet the rules for UK passports.
