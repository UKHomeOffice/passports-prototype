What to ask for at a photo shop

You can get a digital passport photo from most high street photo shops. You'll need to ask them to take a digital photo that meets the rules for UK passports.

What you'll get

Some shops will send you an email with your photo attached.
Or, they'll give you a code to enter when you apply.
