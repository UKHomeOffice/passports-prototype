What to ask for at a photo shop

You can get a digital passport photo from most high street photo shops. Ask them to take a digital photo that meets the rules for UK passports.

What you'll get

A photo shop will usually send you an email with a link to start your application. We can access your digital photo automatically.

Or, your digital photo may be attached to the email. You'll need to save it to the device you're using to apply so that you can upload it.
