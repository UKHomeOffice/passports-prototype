To change the spelling or order of your first names, or to drop a first name you need to send:

{{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}
  • your old passport
  • 2 documents from list A

For any other change you need to send:

  • your old passport
  • 1 document from list A and 1 document from list B

List A

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

List B

  • marriage certificate
  • civil partnership certificate
  • deed poll
  • change of name deed
  • statuary declaration
  • affidavit

If you've changed your name more than once, you'll need to send proof of every name change.


If your documents aren't in English or Welsh you also need to get a certified translation.
