What you need to do

You need to ask someone to confirm your identity. They can do this online. They don't need to sign a printed photo.

You need to ask someone who:

  • has known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend
    or neighbour
  • works in or is retired from a recognised profession
  • has a current UK passport

You must contact the person

We'll send them an email with your application details and a link to go online. You must contact them before we send the email.

Make sure the person knows your date of birth, including the year.
