Ask someone to confirm your identity

The person confirming your identity must:

  • have known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend
    or neighbour
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have a current UK passport

You need to contact the person

We'll send them an email with all the details but you must contact them first. When you speak to them, make sure they:

  • are happy to confirm your identity
  • can go online to do this in the next few days
  • know your date of birth including the year
