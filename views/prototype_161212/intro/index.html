Before you continue

It usually takes about 3 weeks to get your new passport. If you need a passport urgently, use a different service.

You can still use an old valid passport after you've applied. However, we can't work on your application until we've received your old passport.

Changes

If your name has changed, you need to update your information.

If your face has changed significantly, not including the normal signs of ageing, you need to apply in a different way.
