Apply with online Premium

You'll need to:

  1. 1Choose an appointment to collect your new passport

    The earliest appointment is in 2 days. If you need your passport sooner,
    check the paper Premium (1 day) service.

  2. 2Take a digital passport photo – we'll show you how

    You can also get a digital passport photo from a shop. You'll need to do this before you choose an appointment time.

  3. 3Complete your application online

    You'll need a debit or credit card to pay for your new passport.

  4. 4Collect your new passport

    You'll get your new passport 30 minutes after your appointment.

This is a new service - it's only available for some types of application.


If you're applying for a child passport, you need to use the Fast Track service.
