What you need to send

{{/header}} {{$content}}

Before we can send your new passport we’ll need to see the documents below. We’ll check and return them to you.

Your application will be delayed if you don’t send everything we need.

Send all of the following

{{< hmpo-partials-form}} {{$form}}
  • your old passport
  • your birth certificate
  • a signed and dated letter saying you're using your birth name for all purposes
  • your marriage certificate

You also need to send 1 document in your new name

  • utility bill
  • bank statement
  • letter from your employer
  • driving licence
  • medical card
  • letter from your local council or a government department

You must send original documents - we can’t accept photocopies. If you don’t have your original birth or marriage certificate you must get a replacement.
