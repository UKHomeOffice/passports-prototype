Rules for digital passport photos

You’ll need a digital photo to renew your passport online. Your application will be delayed if your photo doesn’t meet these rules.

We’ll crop the photo for you

To do this correctly, photos must:

  1. include your full head, shoulders and upper body
  2. have lots of clear space around your head and shoulders
  3. be taken against a plain background (for example, light grey or cream)
  4. not have any shadows on your face or behind you


In your photo, you must:

  1. be facing forward and looking straight at the camera
  2. have a plain expression and your mouth closed
  3. have your eyes open and visible
  4. not have hair covering your eyes
  5. not have any headwear (unless it's for religious or medical reasons)

    6. Don’t wear sunglasses or tinted glasses. If you wear reading glasses that you can’t take off, your eyes must be visible without any glare or reflections.

Photo quality

Photots must be:

  1. in focus without any shadows
  2. in colour without any effects or filters
  3. at least 600 pixels wide and 750 pixels tall
  4. at least 50KB and no more than 10MB
