If you’re changing your name before the ceremony

You need to send:

  • your old passport
  • a completed passports for newly weds and civil partners form

If you’re changing your name after the ceremony

You need to send:

  • your old passport
  • your marriage or civil partnership certificate

You must send original documents - we can’t accept photocopies. If you don’t have your original marriage certificate you must get a replacement.
