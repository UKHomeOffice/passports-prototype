Check your photo

{{/header}} {{$content}}

Before you submit your photo, check that it meets all of these rules.

  1. Your appearance

    Your photo must be a good likeness taken in the last month.

    In your photo, you must:

    • be facing forward and looking straight at the camera
    • have a plain expression with your mouth closed
    • not have hair covering your eyes
    • have your eyes open and visible (without reflection or glare on glasses)
    • not have any headwear (unless it’s for religious or medical reasons)
  2. Photo set-up

    Your photo must have:

    • a light-coloured background showing a clear outline of your head
    • a plain background without patterns or texture
    • no shadows on your face or behind you
    • no other objects or people in it
  3. Photo quality

    Your photo must:

    • be in colour without any effects or filters
    • not be blurred or have red eye
    • not be edited – you can’t correct this photo digitally

If you submit a photo that doesn't meet these rules, we'll ask you for a different one. This will delay your application.
