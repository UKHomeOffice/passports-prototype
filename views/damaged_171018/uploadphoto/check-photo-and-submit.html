If your photo doesn't meet all of the rules, you'll need to provide a different one. This will delay your application.

  1. Your appearance

    Your photo must be a good likeness taken in the last month.

    Check that:

    • you have a plain expression with your mouth closed
    • your whole face is visible with hair brushed away from your forehead
    • your eyes are open and free from reflection or glare from glasses
    • you're not wearing headwear unless it's for religious or medical reasons
  2. Photo set-up

    Your photo must have:

    • no other objects or people in it
    • a light-coloured background without any texture or patterns
    • no shadows on your face or behind you
  3. Photo quality

    Your photo must:

    • be in colour without any effects or filters
    • not be blurred or have red eye
