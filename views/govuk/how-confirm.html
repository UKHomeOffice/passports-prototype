3. How to confirm someone's identity

This is similar to providing a countersignature but you can do it online.

To confirm someone's identity, you need to:

  1. Be given an application reference and a link from the person applying
  2. Go online using the link and enter the person's application reference
  3. Provide a few details including your UK passport number, national insurance number and employer's details
  4. Identify a photo as the person applying
  5. Confirm that you know the person well

This should take about 10 minutes.
