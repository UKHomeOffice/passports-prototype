Who can do this

You need to ask someone who:

  • has known you for at least 2 years
  • is able to identify you, for example as a friend, neighbour or colleague (not just someone you know professionally, like a doctor)
  • works in or is retired from a recognised profession
  • lives in the UK
  • has a current UK passport

Who are you going to ask?

You need to contact this person and ask for their UK passport number.
