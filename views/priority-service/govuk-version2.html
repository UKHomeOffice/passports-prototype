1. Choose a service

If you're applying for a first British passport or to renew a child passport, you'll need to use a different service.

Get a passport in 1 day

You can complete a paper form or apply online.

Complete a paper form

You can get a paper form at the Post Office. Take your completed form and 2 printed photos to a passport office. You can usually collect your new passport 4 hours after your appointment.

Make an appointment to drop off your form and photos.

Apply online

You can apply online, upload a digital photo and make an appointment to collect your new passport from a passport office. This is a new service. You can usually collect your new passport 1-2 days after submitting your application.

Make an appointment to collect your new passport.

Get a passport in 7 days

You can get a paper form at the Post Office. Take your completed form and 2 printed photos to a passport office. We'll post your new passport to you within 7 days of your appointment. You'll need to be home to sign your new passport when it arrives.

Make an appointment to drop off your form and photos.

In exceptional circumstances, you can upgrade an existing application without making an appointment. Call the Passport Adviceline
