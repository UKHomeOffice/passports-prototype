1. Choose a service

There are 3 ways to get a passport urgently.

You'll need to make and attend an appointment at a passport office in the UK.

Premium 1-day

Take a completed paper application to a passport office. You can usually collect your new passport 4 hours after your appointment.

You can get a paper application form at the Post Office. You'll also need 2 printed photos and supporting documents.

Use the Premium 1-day service.

Premium Click and Collect

Apply online and upload a digital photo. You can usually collect your passport within 2 days of submitting your application.

You'll need to make an appointment to collect your new passport from a passport office. You'll need to take your old passport with you.

This is a new service not yet available to everyone. Check if you can use the Premium Click and Collect service.

Fast Track 7-day

Take a completed paper application to a passport office. We'll post your new passport to you within 7 days of your appointment. You'll need to be home to sign your new passport when it arrives.

You can get a paper application form at the Post Office. You'll also need 2 printed photos and supporting documents.

Use the Fast Track 7-day service.

In exceptional circumstances, you can upgrade an existing application without making an appointment. Call the Passport Adviceline
