Who can do this

The person confirming your identity must:

  • have known you as a friend, neighbour or colleague for at least 2 years
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have a current UK passport

What they need

They'll need some of your application details and a link to go online. You can email them or we can do it for you.

You must contact the person before sharing your application details.
