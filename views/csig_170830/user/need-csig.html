Ask someone to confirm
your identity

Your passport application is on hold. You look very different in your old and new photos.

You need to ask someone you know well to confirm your identity. They can do this online. They don't need to sign a printed photo.

Who can do this

You need to ask someone who:

  • has known you as a friend, neighbour or colleague for at
    least 2 years
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have a current UK passport

What you need to do

  1. Contact the person to get their agreement
  2. Contact the person to get their agreement
  3. Contact the person to get their agreement

You can only use a different title if it’s written on your passport.
