Ask someone to confirm
your identity

You need to do this as your passport is damaged.

You must ask someone who:

  • has known you as a friend, neighbour or colleague for at least 2 years
  • works in or is retired from a recognised profession
  • has a current UK passport

The person confirming your identity needs:

  • your date of birth: {{values.age-day}} {{values.age-month}} {{values.age-year}}
  • your application reference: {{values.reference}}
  • their passport and national insurance numbers
  • this link: http://www.gov.uk/confirm-identity
