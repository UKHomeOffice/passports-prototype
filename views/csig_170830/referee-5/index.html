To confirm someone's identity for a UK passport, you must:

  • have a current UK passport
  • work in or be retired from a recognised profession
  • have known the person as a friend, neighbour or colleague for at least 2 years

We'll ask you a few questions then show you a photo of the person. You don't need to sign a printed photo.

This takes about 10 minutes.
