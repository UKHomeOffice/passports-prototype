We’ll compare your old and new photos when we receive your application.

If we can’t recognise you as the same person, you’ll need to provide a ‘countersignature’.

What’s a countersignature?

A countersignature is when a professional person signs a form to confirm who you are and that the information you’ve given is correct.

If you don’t think you’d be recognised, you’ll need to use a different service that allows you to provide a countersignature.
