Ensure even light and few shadows

There shouldn’t be any shadows on your face or behind your head. Make sure:

  • you use natural light if possible (eg a large window facing you) or a lamp that’s not overhead
  • the lighting is even – don’t stand under a lamp , but do stay inside it's beam
