Before you continue

It usually takes about 3 weeks to get your new passport. Use a different service if you need your passport urgently.

If you're applying for a child passport or for someone unable to make their own decisions, you need to use a different service.

If your face has changed significantly due to an illness or accident, you need to apply in a different way.
