Get a passport urgently

{{/header}} {{$content}}

To get a passport urgently, you'll need to attend an appointment at a passport office in the UK.

Get a passport in 1 day

A standard adult passport costs £128 using this service.

Apply with a paper form

1. Get a paper form at the Post Office.
2. Get 2 printed passport photos from a photo shop.
3. Book an appointment to drop off your completed form and photos.
4. Collect your new passport 4 hours later.

Apply online

1. Book an appointment to collect your new passport.
2. Complete an application online.
3. Take and upload a digital photo - we'll show you how.
4. Attend your appointment to collect your new passport.

This is a new service, there are a limited number of appointments.

Get a passport in 7 days

A standard adult passport costs £103 using this service.

1. Get a paper form at the Post Office.
2. Get 2 printed passport photos from a photo shop.
3. Book an appointment to drop off your completed form and photos.
4. We'll post your new passport to you within 7 days of your appointment.

You'll need to be home to sign your new passport when it arrives.

In exceptional circumstances, you can upgrade an existing application without making an appointment. Call the Passport Adviceline
