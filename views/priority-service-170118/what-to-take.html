3. What to take to your appointment

You'll need to take different documents depending on the service.

Premium 1-day and Fast Track 7-day

You need to take:

  • 2 identical passport photos
  • a completed paper application form from the Post Office
  • your supporting documents - details are in the booklet that comes with the paper form

If you can't go to your appointments

Somebody else can drop off your application, photos and documents.

If you use Premium 1-day or Fast Track 7-day, somebody else can collect your passport for you. They need to take:

  • their ID (passport, driving licence or a utility bill less than 3 months old)
  • a signed and dated letter from you, naming them and giving them permission to collect the passport
  • the application receipt (received when the cashier accepts the application)

Premium Click and Collect

  • You only need to take your old passport with you. We'll cancel it when you collect your new passport. You'll need to collect your new passport in person.
